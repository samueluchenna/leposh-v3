The Le Poshe Story

Founded by a banker turned educator. Led by her still.

Le Poshe began with a question Mrs. Ronke Adeniyi could not stop asking. Twenty years later, it is the answer she lives.

01 / The Founder
Mrs. Ronke Adeniyi
Founder & Director · "RonkePosh"

She is, on paper, an unlikely school proprietress: a Biochemistry graduate of Queen Mary, University of London. A Hull MBA. A decade in London's investment banks — Barclays, Santander, RBS, Citibank. The trajectory was set.

And then she came home, and she chose children.

Mrs. Adeniyi qualified as a Montessori Directress at the Modern Montessori Institute, London, took her Post Graduate Diploma in Education, certified as a Family Life Therapist and NLP Practitioner, and built Le Poshe — a school for the children she would have wanted her own to attend. She has since trained hundreds of teachers across Lagos. She has been featured on NTA, TVC, The Guardian, and The Nation. She is the author of Right Kid 101, a guide for the 21st-century Nigerian child. She runs the Parent Right community of over ten thousand intentional Nigerian parents.

She is at the school every morning. She knows every child by name.
